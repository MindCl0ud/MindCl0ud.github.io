Bersii Recyle App

Bersii is an application that facilitates waste pick-up services and product refills to make your daily life easier. with this app user can order to refill products available like liquid soap or cereal, so customers don't have to go to buy refill products at wholesale stores, with this the customer also reduces the use of single-use plastic waste
